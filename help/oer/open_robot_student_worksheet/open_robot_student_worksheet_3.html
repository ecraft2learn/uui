Open robot project III

TEAM:

We make a moving robot!

We make a robot that moves on wheels. What do you want the robot to look like? co-decide it as a group and paint it here.

1.Build your robot with the materials and tools that you will find in the lab. Take a picture of it during the process and and save selected photos into your group folder.

Tip! Use 2 servomotors to rotate the robot wheels.
Tip! Use the solar bank to power the servomotors.

2.Program your robot to move back and forth

3.Program your robot so that it sees your hand, it stops or turns back.

Tip! Connect a distance sensor properly to the Arduino board so the robot can see obstacles.

4. Create the same robot virtually on the Snap4Arduino screen and make it identify obstacles like the real robot!

Get prepared to present your current work to the rest of the teams.

Do not forget to write down in your calendar!

Open robot project I

TEAM:

We read your calendars! The idea for this project is yours! We make "a robot that moves and is controlled by us".

1.How do you want the robot to look like? How will it move? Decide together with your group and paint it here.

2.Collect the materials and tools you need by taking advantage of what's in the lab. Take a picture of your work during the process and save selected photos into your group folder.

Tip! If you use wheels, use 2 servomotors to rotate the robot wheels.

3.It is basic for the robot to move back and forth Can you do that?

4.Try to create the same robot on the Snap4Arduino screen and make it move back and forth.

5.Get prepared to present your current work to the rest of the teams. Do not forget to write down in your calendar!

Open robot project II

TEAM:

We animate the robot with sensors!

1.If your robot had eyes to see obstacles and distances, what would you like it to be able to do? Decide together as a team and write it here...

2.Position the distance sensor on your robot appropriately.

3.Program your robot with ARDUBLOCK so that it does what you want it to do.

4.Make pictures of your robot and save selected photos into your group folder.

5.What other characteristics would you like your robot to have? Write them here...

6.Continue with the 3D printing of a small accessory that you will place on your robot. Attention! Make sure it does not take too much time to print.

7.Get prepared to present your current work to the other groups. Do not forget to write down your calendar!

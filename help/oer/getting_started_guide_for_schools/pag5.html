Getting Started Guide for Schools http://www.createeducation.com Before introducing 3D printing to your students, if the technology and processes are completely new to you, you may benefit from learning more about the process and workflow involved in creating 3D prints. Understanding the 3D Printing process Our CREATE ambassador Steve Cox has published a video tutorial that guides you through this process. It explains step by step how to go from design to physical object and helping to take out the mystery surrounding 3D Printing. http://www.createeducation.com/resource/3d-printing-workflow/ Introducing 3D Printing to your students It is beneficial to introduce 3D printing to your students before embarking on any 3D printing projects, this will allow them to become familiar with the process, engage and inspire them.

Getting Started Guide for Schools http://www.createeducation.com The first thing you need to do is unpackage and set up your Ultimaker. To do this you can either consult the “Quick Start Guide” that is packaged in your Ultimaker Box, or follow the step-by-step setup guide on the Ultimaker App. There are also some great videos and tutorials on the CREATE Education website which provide a more visual guide to some of the printing processes: Links • http://www.createeducation.com/help-support/videos-tutorials/ • http://www.createeducation.com/resource/ultimaker-original-a-quick-start-guide/ • http://www.createeducation.com/resource/ultimaker-2-quick-start-guide-1/ • http://www.createeducation.com/resource/ultimaker-2-quick-start-guide-part-2/ Setting up your Ultimaker Your very first print To try out your printer and print your very first object, the SD Card provided with your printer has two lovely test print files on. My favourite is the Ultimaker Robot, this is a relatively quick print (approx 30 mins) and makes a nice keyring attachment keepsake. You might want to set the print going at the beginning of a lesson with your students so they can see the finished robot before the end of the lesson and take it in turns to visit the printer to watch it in action.

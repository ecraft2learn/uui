A.I.

TEAM:

We're talking to the robot and its listening to us!

1.If your robot could hear you and understand you, what would you like it to be able to do? Decide together as a team and write it here...

2.Attach the microphone to RaspberryPi.

3.Ask your instructor how to program the robot to listen to your voice commands.

4.Paint a figure on Snap4Arduino and program it so that it can listen to your commands and do what you want.

5.Do something similar with your real robot ...

6.Make a picture of your robot and save selected photos in your group folder. What other charactericts would you like your robot to get? Write them here....

7.Continue with thr 3D printing of a small accessory of the robot Attention! Make sure your printing is not time-consuming.

8. Get prepared to present your current work to the rest of the teams. Do not forget to write down in your calendar!

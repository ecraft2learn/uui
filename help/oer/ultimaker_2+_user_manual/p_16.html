16 To install the Ultimaker 2+: PLACING SPOOL HOLDER 1. Take the spool holder and insert the top part in the hole at the back of the Ultimaker 2+. 2. Push the spool holder down until it snaps into place. PLACING GLASS PLATE 1. Open up the two build plate clamps at the front of the build plate. 2. Gently slide the glass plate onto the build plate and make sure it snaps into the build plate clamps at the back. 3. Close the two build plate clamps at the front to secure the glass plate.

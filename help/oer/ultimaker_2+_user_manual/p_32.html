32 REMOVING THE NEW FILAMENT 1. Lower the temperature to 90 degrees (for PLA) or 110 degrees (for ABS). 2. Pull the filament out with a quick, firm pull, once the temperature has been reached. 3. Check the color and shape of the tip of the filament; the goal is to have a clean, cone-shaped tip. 4. Repeat “heating up and preparing” and “removing the new filament” until the filament comes out without any residue and has a cone-shaped tip. RE-ASSEMBLING 1. Insert the bowden tube through the tube coupling collet all the way down into the TFM coupler. 2. Place the clamp clip around the tube coupling collet so that it secures the bowden tube.

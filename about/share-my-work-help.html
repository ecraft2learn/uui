Share My Work

Share My Work is a tool that enables you to share your own work with your peers as well as see your peers shared work. On the interface you will see three important parts:

  • The share button, which enables you to upload your own work.
  • The 'My Work' tab, which is where you can quickly access your own shared work.
  • The 'Public Work' tab, which is where you will be able to see others uploaded works.

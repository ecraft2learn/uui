Tinkercad

Tinkercad is a free web-based app for creating 3D models suitable for 3D printing. Users create models via the Tinkercad interface, in constrast to BeetleBlocks which supports creating programs that construct 3D models.

Tinkercad can also be used to construct electronic circuits connected to Arduinos and simulate their running.

The eCraft2Learn project is investigating the possibility of providing support and integration of this tool.
